Natural Health Health care app

Project duration 15weeks

My role UX/UI Designer

What is Natural Health

Natural is a responsive web app that provides users with an accessible way to manage their health and access important educational content, which includes Natural remedies.

Whats the target audience?

The Natural health app is targeted to individuals who prioritize natural health and wellness practices and seek a solution to manage their health care needs. This includes people who prefer natural remedies or people who want an easy way to monitor their progress.

Why is an app like Natural Health needed?

Many people find it hard to take care of their health and get trustworthy information about natural remedies and holistic health practices. The usual healthcare system mostly focuses on regular medicine, leaving a gap for a web app like "Natural Health." This app would be easy to use, help people manage their health better, and provide reliable information about natural ways to stay healthy.

Pain points

1

Finding reliable information: People may have difficulty finding reliable information about natural remedies, especially if they are not familiar with the topic.

2

Forgetting appointments: People struggle to remember important appointments related to their health, especially if they have multiple appointments or are managing complex health issues.

3

Keeping track of health data: Many people struggle to keep track of their health data over time, including factors such as physical activity, sleep patterns, and mental health.

Key insights

1

Users want a simple and intuitive system for tracking their health data over time.

2

Users value reminders and notifications to help them keep track of important appointments related to their health and wellness.

3

Users want access to reliable information about natural remedies, presented in a way that is easy to understand and apply to their own health and wellness journey.

Mobile first

I created a mobile-first app because of the research results I got from the surveys, interview and background research. By starting with the mobile version the design and functionality are optimized for smaller screens and touch-based interactions. This approach also allows for easier scalability to larger devices and desktop versions of the app. By designing for mobile-first also allows me to make sure that the needs of most users who use the app on their mobile devices are taken care of.

Usability test

I conducted usability tests on my design to gain valuable feedback from users and improve the user experience. By observing user behavior and identifying design flaws, I aimed to enhance the overall usability of my design.

Background information to the usability study

Background

Natural Health is an app that helps people track their physical and mental health. You can use it to learn about health topics, schedule events, and store information about your health. The app is designed for people who want to take care of their overall wellbeing in a personalized way.

Test objectives

  • Evaluate the usability by asking the participants to complete tasks
  • Identify confusions in navigation
  • Identify challenges and problems when the participant is using the app
  • Evaluate user satisfaction

Methodology

  • Usability test is Moderated in person (4 participants) or remote (2 participants)
  • Participants will be contacted by text message and then get the details per email
  • Before starting the test, the user will get an introduction of the App and process of the study
  • The participant can ask questions before starting the test
  • Participant will complete tasks and then then get some follow up questions

Goal

The usability test aims to test the effectiveness of the app's current design which includes evaluating whether the app's features and functionality are meeting the needs of users. By testing the effectiveness of the design, the usability test can help identify areas where the app can be optimized and improved to better serve its users.

Sessions

Every session will take 5 to 10 minutes for the introduction of the study, around 15 minutes for the test and additional 10 minutes for the follow up questions.

Equipment

The test will be conducted on an iPhone. For the remote study a laptop is needed. The notes will be taken with a pencil and notebook

Schedule

The test will be held between May 7, May 15, 2023 The location depends on the participant (In person or remote)
