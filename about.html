About me

Hi, I'm Romina and I bring a unique blend of creativity, discipline, and empathy to the world of UX design. My journey started with a passion for traditional painting, where I honed my artistic skills and learned to see the world through a different lens, which helps me to improved visual communication in my UX design process and creative problem-solving  Currently, I'm pursuing my studies in graphic design while juggling a dynamic role as a bartender. The bartending experience has taught me the importance of connecting with people from all walks of life and understanding their diverse needs. While Graphic design helps me in UX design by visually communicating information and creating a intuitive interface enhancing the user's understanding, engagement, and overall experience with the product or service. I thrive on energy and am known for my self-discipline, which helps me tackle design challenges with determination. My responsible and independent nature ensures that I can deliver results even in high-pressure situations. In my free time I peruse my long time hobby- the analogue photography, which helps me to train my eye in composition and color. What sets me apart as a UX designer is my ability to merge my artistic sensibilities with a user-centric approach. I'm passionate about researching the background of an app and then creating visually appealing solutions. My goal is to help clients not only enhance their user experiences but also drive revenue growth and expand their client base.  If you're looking for a UX designer who brings a fresh perspective, creative flair, and a strong commitment to user satisfaction, let's connect. I'm excited to collaborate with you and create memorable digital experiences.
