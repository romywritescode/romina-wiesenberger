Project Objective

This app should help users to create beautiful DIY home projects. The app should help to find projects, explain them in a video and give the user a material list, so they know exactly what to buy. A nice extra feature will be the community, where users can share their projects, ask for help of even create a group to connect with other people who enjoy doing DIY projects.

User Persona

By creating user personas, I aimed to ensure that the product or service was designed and marketed in a way that resonates with the target audience, resulting in higher engagement and satisfaction.

Meet Sarah

  • Age: 35
  • Gender: Female
  • Relationship: Married with a 6 year old chilz
  • Occupation: Full-time office manager
  • Education: Bachelor s degree in Business Administratio1
  • Location: Germany

Goals and Needs:

  • Sarah loves DIY home decor projects and enjoys decorating her home on a budget
  • She wants to feel the sense of community and create friendships through passion

Motivations:

  • Sarah is motivated by people who think likewise. She likes the interact with people and gets motivated by the support
  • She is also motivated by the fact that her daughter will grow up in a home decorated by herself.

Frustations:

  • Sarah is frustrated by the lack of time to research and plan DIY projects due to her busy schedule and her small child
  • She is also frustrated by feeling alone and not be able to ask for help
  • Sarah is frustrated by the risk of buying the wrong items.

Problem Statement

Engaging in DIY home decor projects can be challenging and time-consuming, particularly for beginners who lack the skills, knowledge, and resources to complete projects. It also can be frustrating to be unsure what materials to buy and not be able to ask for help.

Possible Solutions

The DIY home decor app will provide a user-friendly way to engage in DIY home decor projects, saving them time and providing value-added features. The app will offer inspiration, tutorials, material lists, project and a community of users for a great user experience.
